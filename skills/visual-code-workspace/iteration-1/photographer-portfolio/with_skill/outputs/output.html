About Elena

Elena Vasquez is a Barcelona-born photographer based in New York City, working in landscape, portrait, and fine art. With over a decade behind the lens, her work has been exhibited in galleries across Europe and North America, and published in international editorial titles.

She brings a documentary instinct to every project — chasing light, texture, and quiet moments of honesty. Available for editorial commissions, brand campaigns, and private portrait sessions worldwide.
