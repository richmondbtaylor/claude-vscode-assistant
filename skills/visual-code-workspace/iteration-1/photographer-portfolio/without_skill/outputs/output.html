About Elena

Elena Vasquez is a Barcelona-born photographer based in New York City, specializing in landscape, portrait, and fine art photography. With over a decade behind the lens, her work has been exhibited internationally and published in leading editorial titles.

She approaches every shoot with a documentary instinct — capturing light, texture, and emotion in their most authentic form. Available for editorial assignments, brand campaigns, and private commissions worldwide.
