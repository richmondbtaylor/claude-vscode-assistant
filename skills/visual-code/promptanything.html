CRISPE Most popular

Capacity · Role · Insight · Statement · Personality · Experiment

The gold standard for complex AI tasks. CRISPE tells the model exactly who it is, what it knows, and how to think — producing expert-level output from the first try.

Best for: Research, writing, analysis
Complexity: Advanced
CLEAR Beginner friendly

Concise · Logical · Explicit · Adaptive · Reflective

Simple, direct, effective. CLEAR is the fastest way to get clean, usable output without over-engineering your prompt. Perfect when you need results now.

Best for: Quick tasks, summaries
Complexity: Beginner
STAR Structured

Situation · Task · Action · Result

Borrowed from interview methodology, STAR grounds AI output in real-world context — situation first, then task, action, and expected result. Eliminates vague responses.

Best for: Business cases, scenarios
Complexity: Intermediate
BRAINSTORM Creative

Background · Role · Agenda · Inspiration · Nature · Style · Tone · Output · Reasoning · Method

The most comprehensive creative framework. BRAINSTORM unlocks maximum creative output by loading the model with full context before generating a single word.

Best for: Creative writing, campaigns
Complexity: Advanced
SOPS Process-driven

Standard Operating Prompt Structure

Built for repeatable, consistent outputs. SOPS is ideal when you need the same quality result every time — perfect for teams, workflows, and automation pipelines.

Best for: SOPs, docs, workflows
Complexity: Intermediate
