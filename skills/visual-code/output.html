Everything you need to know about PromptPerfect.

PromptPerfect is an AI-powered prompt optimization tool built by Jina AI. It analyzes your raw prompt, understands your intent, and automatically rewrites it using best practices for your target AI model — adding context, structure, constraints, and clarity that consistently produce better outputs. Simply paste your prompt, select your target model, and get an optimized version in seconds.

PromptPerfect supports 10+ leading AI models including ChatGPT (GPT-4, GPT-3.5), Claude (2 & 3), Google Gemini, Midjourney, DALL-E, Stable Diffusion, Llama 2, Cohere, Mistral, and more. New models are added regularly. Each optimization is model-specific — what works best for Claude differs from what works best for Midjourney.

The free plan is genuinely useful for casual users — 20 optimizations per month covers most hobbyist or light professional use. You get access to 3 AI models and basic prompt history. No credit card required to sign up. If you hit the limit regularly, that's usually a sign the Pro plan will pay for itself quickly in time saved.

Your prompts are stored securely to power your personal prompt history feature and are never shared with third parties or used to train public AI models. You can delete your prompt history at any time from your account settings. Enterprise customers can opt for zero data retention, where prompts are processed and immediately discarded.

The REST API lets you send prompts programmatically and receive optimized versions, making it easy to embed PromptPerfect into your own AI-powered products, internal tools, or automation workflows. Full API documentation is available in your dashboard after signup.
