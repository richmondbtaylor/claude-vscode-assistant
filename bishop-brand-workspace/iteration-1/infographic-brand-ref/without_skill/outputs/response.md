## Recommended Color Palette

For an AI company infographic, a clean, professional palette typically leans on deep blues or dark neutrals as a foundation, with accent colors that signal intelligence, trust, and innovation.

### Primary Colors

| Role | Color Name | Hex Code | Usage |
|---|---|---|---|
| Primary Brand | Deep Navy | `#0D1B2A` | Backgrounds, headers, primary text |
| Brand Blue | Electric Blue | `#1A73E8` | Primary accent, key stats, highlights |
| Brand Highlight | Cyan / Teal | `#00C2CC` | Secondary accent, icons, data callouts |

### Secondary / Supporting Colors

| Role | Color Name | Hex Code | Usage |
|---|---|---|---|
| Light BG | Off White | `#F4F6F9` | Section backgrounds, card fills |
| Mid Grey | Slate Grey | `#6B7A8D` | Body text, captions, secondary labels |
| Dark Text | Charcoal | `#1C2B3A` | Body copy, subheadings |
| Success/Growth | Emerald Green | `#00A86B` | Positive metrics (e.g., % improvements) |
| Energy/Contrast | Amber Orange | `#F5A623` | Call-to-action elements, number highlights |
| White | Pure White | `#FFFFFF` | Text on dark backgrounds, card backgrounds |

## Recommended Typography

### Primary Font (Headings & Stats)
- **Font Name:** Inter
- **Weights:** Bold (700), Extra Bold (800)
- **Usage:** Section titles, large stat numbers (e.g., "300% ROI"), infographic headline
- **Source:** Google Fonts (free)

### Secondary Font (Body & Labels)
- **Font Name:** Inter
- **Weights:** Regular (400), Medium (500)
- **Usage:** Descriptions, captions, footnotes, client names

### Alternative Option
- **Heading Font:** Space Grotesk Bold (700) — geometric, tech-forward feel
- **Body Font:** Inter Regular (400)

## Layout Tips for the "Top 5 Results" Format

1. Use Deep Navy (#0D1B2A) for the main header background with white headline text.
2. Feature each of the 5 results in a card with an Off White (#F4F6F9) or white background.
3. Display the key metric in a large Inter ExtraBold font, colored with Electric Blue (#1A73E8) or Emerald Green (#00A86B).
4. Use Cyan (#00C2CC) for icons or numbered indicators (1-5) to create visual rhythm.
5. Place a thin Amber (#F5A623) accent line on the most impressive result to draw the eye.
